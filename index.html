Behind The Lens

Crafting Content One Frame at a Time

I fell in love with cameras at the young age of 8 and instantly became infatuated with documenting timeless moments. Over my 6 year professional career, I’ve captured numerous weddings, sporting events, branding content, and professional events. Being equally yolked in videography as well as photography has enabled me to provide a wide variety of services that bring my client’s ideas to fruition.

Being a proud alumni of Prairie View A&M University, where I attained my degree in Mass Communications as well as Marketing, I attained the proper skills to not only curate quality content but conduct business at a commercial level. I currently freelance in the surrounding Houston area in addition to working as a Custom Content Contributor for Getty Images where I shoot marketing content for brands like AT&T, Oncor, Bank of America, MLB, 3M, Chipotle, NCAA and more.
